Buena Vista Lakes


About our Neighborhood

Buena Vista is a lake community located in Hernando, MS. There are two main lakes in the neighborhood. One is called Rain Cloud Lake and the other is Thunderbird Lake. My family and I live on Thunderbird Lake and we love our view of the lake from our back deck. We have tons of geese here throughout the year, and every spring they have babies. We love watching the baby geese grow up throughout the spring and summer. Everyone in our neighborhood is friendly, and when you drive through everyone waves and smiles at each other. We love it out here!
